Learn German with Music: Max Raabe & Palast Orchester

This must-do experience for fans of the ‘fab four’ explores the journey of how The Beatles became worldwide superstars. The award-winning The Beatles Story attraction is dedicated to the lives and times of the world’s biggest pop group, and is located in their hometown of Liverpool. Be transported on an incredible journey and see how these four young lads were propelled to the dizzy heights of fame and fortune from their humble childhood beginnings. Using the sights and sounds of the 1950s and 60s, visitors are transported from Liverpool via Hamburg to the USA, following The Beatles meteoric rise to stardom
